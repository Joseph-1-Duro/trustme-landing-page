HOW IT WORKS

Simple. Secure. Transparent.

Every property deal is protected with our 3-step escrow process — ensuring funds and documents are only released when all parties are satisfied.

Step 1: Deposit

The buyer deposits the agreed funds into a secure TrustMe escrow wallet. Both parties are notified instantly, ensuring the money is safely held.

Step 2: Verification

TrustMe verifies documents, titles, and compliance (KYC/AML). Once the buyer and seller meet their conditions, the system flags the transaction as ready.

Step 3: Release

Once all conditions are verified, TrustMe automatically releases the funds to the seller — closing the deal with transparency and trust.
